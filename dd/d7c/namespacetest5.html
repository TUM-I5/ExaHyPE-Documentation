Detailed Description

This test checks that R * A_DG * P * u_random = A_CG * u_random and R * M_DG * P * u_random = M_CG * u_random for some random vector u_random.

Here R and P are the restriction and prolongation operators, respectively, A_DG, A_CG are system matrices and M_DG, M_CG are mass matrices for DG and CG, respectively.
