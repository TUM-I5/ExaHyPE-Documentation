Detailed Description

Provide meta data around Swift 2 cell.

Lightweight wrapper around a couple of attributes which have to be passed into particle (pair) update kernels. I started to add more and more attributes. Over time, I got fed up with all the variants and therefore introduce this lightweight wrapper around attributes. Due to this this one, we can extend the attributes passed in at any point without having to touch each individual piece of source code.
