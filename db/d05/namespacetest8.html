Detailed Description

This test checks that A_CG indeed approximates the continuum operator: check A_DG * u_exact == M * b_exact, where u_exact and b_exact are the dof-vectors of the functions u_exact(x) and b_exact(x) which satisfy the PDE in the continuum.

  • Laplace u_exact(x) = b_exact(x).

We use u_exact(x,y) = sin(2*pi*x) * sin(2*pi*y), b_exact(x,y) = 8*pi^2 * sin(2*pi*x) * sin(2*pi*y)
