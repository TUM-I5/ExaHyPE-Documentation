◆ create_data_structures()

exahype2.solvers.limiting.PosterioriLimiting.PosterioriLimiting.create_data_structures ( self)

This will serve as a face marker to communicate with neighbouring cells whether a given cell is troubled, is neighbouring a troubled cell, or is fine.

Cells that are troubled or neighbouring a troubled cell must rollback their solution to the previous one and perform one time step using a more robust FV scheme. Therefore these and their neighbours must convert the DG representation of their previous solution to an FV representation and project it to their faces so that these can be exchanged with their neighbours.
