11class ComputeFinalLinearCombination(AbstractRKFDActionSet):
12 r"""!
13
14 We solve the ODE
15
16 @f$ \partial Q = F(Q) @f$
17
18 where we have multiple guesses for F according to the Butcher tableau. The final
19 solution can be written down as linear combination over these guesses:
20
21
22 @f$ Q^{new} = Q^{old} + a_1 \cdot dt \cdot F_1(Q) + a_2 \cdot dt \cdot F_2(Q) + \dots @f$
23
24 We observe the following simple facts:
25
26 - The data in fineGridCell{{UNKNOWN_IDENTIFIER}}.value holds @f$ Q^{old} @f$
27 - The data in fineGridCell{{UNKNOWN_IDENTIFIER}}.value shall hold @f$ Q^{new} @f$ eventually.
28 We thus can simply add the scaled estimated to it.
29 - The estimates do not alter the auxiliary variables.
30 - We can thus just keep the previous auxiliary variables implicitly.
31
32 In the FD4 world, we can integrate the ComputeFinalLinearCombination
33 action set directly into the very last mesh sweep. At this point, all
34 the right-hand side estimates are in place.
35
36
37 ## Invocation order
38
39 It is important to study this action set in combination with ProjectPatchOntoFaces.
40 Both plug into touchCellLastTime(), but the projection has to come before the
41 computation of the final solution. So it has to have a higher order as we invert
42 the order throughout the steps up the tree.
43
44 """
