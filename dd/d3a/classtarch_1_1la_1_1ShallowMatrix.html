template<int Rows, int Cols, typename Scalar>
class tarch::la::ShallowMatrix< Rows, Cols, Scalar >

Shallow matrix wrapping an existing array.

You are in charge of handling the underlying memory, i.e. the class does neither allocate anything nor destroy anything
